Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Anonymous usage statistics to improve the application
  • Crash reports and error data through Sentry may include log files, project configuration files, and dependency information to help diagnose issues. This data may contain project-specific information but is used solely for debugging purposes
  • Basic system information (OS version, app version, device model) for compatibility purposes
  • IP addresses and technical device information when errors occur (collected by Sentry)

You can review and opt out for some other options by opening Settings ‚Üí Privacy.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve the application's performance and reliability
  • Fix bugs and resolve technical issues through Sentry's error tracking
  • Provide better user experience
  • Monitor application stability and identify recurring issues

Data Storage and Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • All application data is stored locally on your device
  • Error and crash data is securely transmitted to Sentry for analysis
  • No personal information is intentionally collected or transmitted
  • Anonymous analytics data is encrypted before transmission
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as necessary for error tracking through Sentry

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to improve our application:

  • Sentry: We use Sentry for error tracking and crash reporting. Sentry collects technical information about errors and crashes to help us identify and fix issues. Sentry is committed to data protection and complies with applicable privacy laws. You can learn more about Sentry's privacy practices at sentry.io/privacy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of anonymous data collection in app settings
  • Disable crash reporting and error tracking through Sentry
  • Delete all cached data at any time
  • Request access to or deletion of data collected through Sentry
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our Contact Us page.
